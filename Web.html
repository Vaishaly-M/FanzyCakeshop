    About Us

    Fanzy Cakes had its beginnings somewhere in 2018, when family friends asked to make cakes for their special days. The response we received was overwhelming and that gradually led us to the idea of starting our own business. We offer a wide range of fresh produce daily, baked in house by our growing team of skilled bakers. Whether you’re looking for a light savoury bite, or something to satisfy your sweet tooth, we’ve got you covered.
