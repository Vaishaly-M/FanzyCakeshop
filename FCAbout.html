    About Us

    Fanzy Cakes had its beginnings somewhere in 2020, when family friends asked to make cakes for their special days. The response we received was overwhelming and that gradually led us to the idea of starting our own business. We offer a wide range of fresh produce daily, baked in house by our growing team of skilled bakers. Whether you’re looking for a light savoury bite, or something to satisfy your sweet tooth, we’ve got you covered.

    Our Blog

    We understand that different people have different preferences when it comes to cakes. That's why we offer a wide range of cakes that are designed to suit the tastes and preferences of different customers.

    Cake by Flavour

    There are countless flavors to choose from. Some popular cake flavorsinclude vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, lemon, strawberry, and many more. Each flavor has its own unique taste and can be customized to suit individual preferences. Whether you prefer a classic vanilla cake or an adventurous fusion flavor, there is a cake out there for everyone. So indulge your sweet tooth and explore the delicious world of cakes by flavor!

    Cake by relations

    At Cakes by Relations, we understand that every celebration is unique, and we work closely with our customers to create custom cakes that reflect their personality and style. We take pride in our ability to bring your vision to life and make your celebration even more special. So whether you're planning a small gathering or a grand event, let Cakes by Relations help you celebrate in style with our delectable cakes.

    Cake by recipient

    We also offer cakes for specific dietary requirements, such as vegan and gluten-free cakes, to ensure that everyone can enjoy our delicious desserts.No matter who the recipient is, we believe that every cake should be made with the same level of care, attention to detail, and quality ingredients. At Cakes by Relations, we are committed to making every cake a special and memorable treat for its recipient.
